= Scenarios

What kinds of usage scenarios will each persona be involved with in order to meet his or her goals?

== Common Scenarios

1. General goal: Assess the accessibility of a web page and address any problems that are identified.
2. More specifically: Find out if there //are// accessibility problems, and if so:
  * How many are there and what are their relative severities?
  * How can I fix the problems and how should I prioritize them?
